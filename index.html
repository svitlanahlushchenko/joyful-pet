About Joyful Pet Cattery

Our main specialization is the production of clematis. Our collection includes more than 450 species and varieties of clematis, but for commercial purposes, we produce just over 280 specimens of clematis. These include large-flowered and small-flowered clematis as well as climbing forms of clematis across a wide range.

We also have a secondary specialty in climbing plants that can be grown in garden conditions in the Polish climate; we produce more than a hundred species and varieties of such plants.
